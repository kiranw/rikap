THEIR STORY

This story starts in neighboring study cubicles in medical school. Kapil was mesmerized by Rima's intelligence and efficiency. Meanwhile Rima was amazed by Kapil's professional talent in procrastinating. They became inseparable study buddies but routinely mixed up their papers because their microscopic handwriting was identical. They adored each others quirky tendencies and friendship blossomed into love after plenty of concert dates and bubble tea in the park.

WILL YOU MARRY ME?

SHE SAID YES !!!

She woke up thinking she was having a long overdue lunch date with Sneha and Shruti but little did she know that the surprise of her life was about to unfold. Shama, Keya, Kiran and Kapil decorated the gazebo overlooking the Manhattan skyline and family and friends gathered holding posters featuring the couple's most memorable moments. With unrivaled stealth and photographic talent, Amrit and Murphy captured each of their sides of the story. Sneha and Shruti brought over Rima, who on the walk, was just as oblivious as everyone hoped, talking about how she thinks Kapil will propose in 6 months. The girls posed for a picture and Kapil snuck into the background, holding up a photo of when they first met.

They girls urged her to see how the photo came out. When she laid eyes on the camera screen, she squealed, gasped, and turned around, confused, to see Kapil wielding a bouquet of the reddest roses. Tears ensued and the soundtrack of their love filled the air, tastefully constructed by Kapil to make her heart melt. They walked through the aisle of posters, celebrating their relationship with family and friends, leading up to the gazebo where Kapil asked a very leading question. Pressured by the number of people there, she had no choice but to say yes. The couple retired to her house where another surprise awaited and they rejoiced with family and friends.
